<p><p>The semiconductor electroplating chemicals market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Pretreatment Agents**: These chemicals prepare the surface of semiconductors for electroplating, enhancing adhesion and uniformity.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Electroplating Additives**: These are essential for modulating the electroplating process, improving deposit quality, and controlling parameters like grain structure and thickness.</p><p>3. **Post-treatment Agents**: Used after plating, these chemicals optimize the properties of the plated surface, enhancing performance and reliability in semiconductor applications. Each type plays a crucial role in the overall electroplating process.</p></p>
