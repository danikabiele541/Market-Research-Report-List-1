<p><p>The dosing pumps market comprises several types, including plunger, mechanical, and hydraulic dosing pumps. Plunger dosing pumps utilize a reciprocating piston to dispense precise fluid volumes, ideal for high-pressure applications. Mechanical dosing pumps rely on diaphragms or gears for fluid transfer, often used in chemical processing. Hydraulic dosing pumps leverage fluid pressure to achieve accurate dosing, suitable for heavy-duty operations. Each type serves specific industry needs, ensuring accurate and efficient fluid handling across various applications.</p></p>
