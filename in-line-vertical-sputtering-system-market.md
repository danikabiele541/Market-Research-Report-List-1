<p><ul><li>In-line Vertical Static Sputtering System</li><li>In-line Vertical Dynamic Sputtering System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The In-line Vertical Sputtering System market is divided into two primary types: In-line Vertical Static Sputtering System and In-line Vertical Dynamic Sputtering System. The static system is designed for batch processing, where substrates remain stationary during the sputtering process, offering high uniformity and control over film thickness. In contrast, the dynamic system involves continuous substrate motion, enhancing throughput and enabling faster production cycles, making it suitable for high-volume manufacturing. Both systems cater to varying industry demands for semiconductor and thin-film applications.</p></p>
