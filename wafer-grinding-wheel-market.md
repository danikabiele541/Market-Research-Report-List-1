<p><p>The wafer grinding wheel market is segmented into two main types: coarse grind and fine grind. Coarse grind wheels are designed for rapid material removal, ideal for initial shaping and reducing thick substrates in semiconductor manufacturing. In contrast, fine grind wheels provide a smoother finish and are used for precision grinding, enhancing surface quality and dimensional accuracy. Both types are crucial in the production of silicon wafers, catering to different stages of the grinding process for effective wafer fabrication.</p></p>
