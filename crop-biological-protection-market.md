<p><strong>The Crop Biological Protection Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Microbial Pesticides</li><li>Biochemical Pesticides</li><li>Plant-Incorporated Protectants (PIPs)</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Crop Biological Protection market encompasses various types of eco-friendly solutions for pest and disease management. **Microbial Pesticides** use microorganisms like bacteria, fungi, or viruses to target specific pests. **Biochemical Pesticides** rely on naturally occurring substances, such as pheromones or plant extracts, to disrupt pest behavior or growth. **Plant-Incorporated Protectants (PIPs)** involve genetically modified crops that produce their own pest-resistant proteins. **Others** include beneficial insects or organic compounds. These methods reduce chemical dependency, promote sustainability, and align with eco-conscious agricultural practices.</p></p>
