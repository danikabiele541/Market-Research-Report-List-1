<p><p>The Distributed Feedback Laser Diode (DFB-LD) is a specialized semiconductor laser that employs a periodic structure within its design to achieve single-mode operation and precise wavelength control. DFB-LDs emit narrow linewidth laser light, making them ideal for long-distance fiber-optic communications.</p><p>The DFB-LD market is anticipated to experience significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 5.7% during the forecast period.
