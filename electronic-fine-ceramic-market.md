<p><p>The Electronic Fine Ceramic Market comprises two main types: Electronic Structural Ceramics and Electronic Functional Ceramics. Electronic Structural Ceramics focus on providing mechanical support and stability in electronic applications, such as substrates and packages for semiconductor devices. In contrast, Electronic Functional Ceramics emphasize specific electrical, magnetic, or optical properties, enabling functionalities like capacitors, piezoelectric sensors, and insulators. Together, these ceramics play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and reliability of electronic components across various industries.</p></p>
