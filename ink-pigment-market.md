<p><p>The ink pigment market is primarily segmented into organic and inorganic pigments. Organic pigments, derived from carbon-based compounds, are known for their vibrant colors and excellent lightfastness, making them popular in high-quality inks. In contrast, inorganic pigments are mineral-based and typically offer better opacity and durability, often used in applications requiring stability and resistance to heat and chemicals. Together, these pigments cater to diverse industries including printing, packaging, and art, enhancing the quality and performance of ink products.</p></p>
