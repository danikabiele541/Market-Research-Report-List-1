Drum-Type models feature cylindrical designs that efficiently transfer heat from exhaust gases to incoming air, enhancing combustion efficiency in boilers. Windshield Rotary Air Preheaters, on the other hand, utilize a distinct geometric design to optimize heat exchange while minimizing energy loss. Both types aim to improve thermal efficiency and reduce emissions in industrial processes, contributing to energy conservation and sustainability.</p></p>
