<p><strong>The Pneumatic Nebulizers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vented Pneumatic Nebulizers</li><li>Breath-Actuated Pneumatic Nebulizers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic nebulizer market comprises primarily two types: vented pneumatic nebulizers and breath-actuated pneumatic nebulizers. Vented pneumatic nebulizers continuously operate, producing a steady aerosol flow regardless of the patient's breathing pattern, making them suitable for various conditions. Conversely, breath-actuated nebulizers activate upon inhalation, optimizing medication delivery while minimizing drug waste during exhalation. This results in improved efficiency and patient compliance. Both nebulizer types cater to diverse needs, enhancing the management of respiratory diseases.</p></p>
