<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Silk Quilt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mulberry Silk</li><li>Tussah Silk</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silk quilts are classified primarily into two market types: Mulberry silk and Tussah silk. Mulberry silk, produced by silkworms that feed exclusively on mulberry leaves, is known for its superior softness, luster, and durability, making it the preferred choice for luxury bedding. In contrast, Tussah silk, derived from wild silkworms, has a coarser texture and a natural, rustic appeal, often favored for eco-friendly and budget-conscious options. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and price points in the silk quilt market.</p></p>
