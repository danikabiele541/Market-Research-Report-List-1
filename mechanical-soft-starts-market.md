<p><strong>The Mechanical Soft Starts Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluid Couplings</li><li>Flexidyne Couplings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The mechanical soft starts market includes technologies like fluid couplings and Flexidyne couplings, which serve to manage startup torque and reduce mechanical stress on equipment. Fluid couplings utilize hydraulic fluid to smoothen the acceleration of motors, ensuring a gradual increase in speed. Flexidyne couplings, on the other hand, employ a viscoelastic material to achieve controlled torque transmission. Both types are essential for applications requiring reduced wear and tear, enhanced efficiency, and increased lifespan of machinery during startup sequences.</p></p>
