<p><ul><li>Short Decay</li><li>Long Decay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The phosphor screen market is divided into two primary types based on decay time: short decay and long decay phosphors. Short decay phosphors emit light swiftly, making them ideal for applications requiring rapid image refresh rates, such as in digital imaging and medical diagnostics. Conversely, long decay phosphors persist in emitting light for extended periods, which is beneficial for applications like night vision devices and glow-in-the-dark screens, where sustained visibility is essential. Each type serves distinct technological needs.</p></p>
