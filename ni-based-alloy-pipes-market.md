<p><p>The Ni-based alloy pipes market is primarily divided into two types: seamless pipes and welded pipes. Seamless pipes are manufactured without joints, ensuring higher strength and resistance to pressure, making them ideal for high-performance applications. On the other hand, welded pipes are created by fusing two metal pieces together, which can be more cost-effective and offer a wider variety of sizes and shapes. Both types cater to industries like oil and gas, aerospace, and chemical processing, depending on specific requirements.</p></p>
