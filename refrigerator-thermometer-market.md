<p><p>The refrigerator thermometer market is divided into two main types: digital and analog thermometers. Digital refrigerator thermometers provide precise temperature readings on an easy-to-read display, often featuring additional functions like alarms for temperature fluctuations. In contrast, analog refrigerator thermometers utilize a dial and a traditional mercury or alcohol-based system to indicate temperature, offering a simple and durable option. Both types serve to monitor and maintain optimal conditions within refrigerators, ensuring food safety and quality.</p></p>
