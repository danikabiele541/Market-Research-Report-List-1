<p><p>The normal headphone market consists of three primary types: over-ear, on-ear, and in-ear headphones. Over-ear headphones enclose the ears for superior sound isolation and comfort, making them ideal for long listening sessions. On-ear headphones rest on the ears, providing a balance of portability and sound quality while remaining lightweight. In-ear headphones, or earbuds, fit directly into the ear canal, offering exceptional portability and noise isolation, making them popular for active lifestyles and commuting. Each type caters to different user preferences and needs.</p></p>
