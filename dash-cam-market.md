<p><p>A dash cam, or dashboard camera, is a compact video recording device mounted on a vehicle's dashboard or windshield to capture real-time footage of the road. Dash cams have gained popularity due to their ability to provide evidence in case of accidents, theft, or disputes, as well as for enhancing driver safety.</p><p>The Dash Cam Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 11.1% during the forecast period. Wireless and dual-lens dash cams are also gaining traction, offering enhanced convenience and coverage. The market is witnessing a shift toward higher-resolution cameras, with 4K dash cams becoming increasingly popular. As urbanization and vehicle ownership rise globally, the dash cam market is poised for sustained growth.</p></p>
