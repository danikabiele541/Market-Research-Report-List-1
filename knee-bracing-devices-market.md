<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Knee Bracing Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Prophylactic Braces</li><li>Functional Braces</li><li>Rehabilitative Braces</li><li>Unloader Braces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Knee bracing devices are classified into four main types: </p><p>1. **Prophylactic Braces** are designed to prevent injuries during high-risk sports activities.  </p><p>2. **Functional Braces** provide support to knees weakened by injury, promoting stability during physical activities.   </p><p>3. **Rehabilitative Braces** aid recovery by limiting harmful movements post-surgery or injury.  </p><p>4. **Unloader Braces** relieve pain in osteoarthritis patients by redistributing weight away from the affected knee compartment.  </p><p>Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to different needs within the knee support market.</p></p>
