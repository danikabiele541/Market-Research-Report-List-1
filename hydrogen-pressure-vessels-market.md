<p><p>Hydrogen pressure vessels are categorized into four types based on their construction and materials. </p><p>Type I vessels are all-metal and offer high strength but are heavy. </p><p>Type II combines metal with a composite overwrap, reducing weight while maintaining strength. </p><p>Type III features a metal liner with a lighter composite shell, enhancing performance and safety. </p><p>Type IV vessels have a plastic liner entirely wrapped with composite materials, maximizing lightweight characteristics, making them ideal for hydrogen storage in various applications.</p></p>
