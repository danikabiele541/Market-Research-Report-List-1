<p><p>The battery market for consumer products primarily comprises three types: Li-Ion, Lead Acid, and Nickel batteries. Lead Acid batteries are known for their cost-effectiveness and robustness, often used in automotive applications. Nickel batteries, including Nickel-Cadmium and Nickel-Metal Hydride, offer reliable performance and longer cycle life, commonly employed in power tools and older electronic devices.
