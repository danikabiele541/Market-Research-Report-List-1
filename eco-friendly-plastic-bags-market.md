<p><p>The eco-friendly plastic bags market encompasses various types, including Polylactic Acid (PLA), Polyhydroxyalkanoate (PHA), and starch blends. PLA is derived from renewable resources like corn starch, offering biodegradability and compostability. PHA, produced by microbial fermentation, is another biodegradable option that breaks down naturally in diverse environments. Starch blends combine starch with other polymers, providing an alternative that is both biodegradable and compostable. Together, these materials represent a sustainable solution to reduce plastic waste and environmental impact.</p></p>
