<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Steam Valve</li><li>Electric Steam Valve</li><li>Self-operated Steam Valve</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The steam valves market comprises three main types: pneumatic, electric, and self-operated steam valves. Pneumatic steam valves utilize compressed air for operation, offering quick response times and reliability in various industrial applications. Electric steam valves are driven by electric actuators, allowing precise control and automation, ideal for complex systems. Self-operated steam valves function without external power, relying on the steam pressure itself for operation, making them suitable for simple, low-maintenance installations. Together, they serve diverse industries, enhancing efficiency and safety.</p></p>
