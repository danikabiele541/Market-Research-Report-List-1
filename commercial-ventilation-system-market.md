<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Commercial Ventilation System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Exhaust Ventilation System</li><li>Supply Ventilation System</li><li>Balanced Ventilation System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial ventilation system market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Exhaust Ventilation System**: Primarily removes stale air from a building, creating a negative pressure that draws in fresh air from outside.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Supply Ventilation System**: Introduces filtered, conditioned air into a space, ensuring a positive pressure environment and improved air quality.</p><p>  </p><p>3. **Balanced Ventilation System**: Combines both exhaust and supply systems, allowing for equal air inflow and outflow, optimizing indoor air quality and comfort. Each system serves distinct operational needs in commercial settings.</p></p>
