<p><p>The metallized base film market encompasses various types, primarily including BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) film, PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) film, and CPP (Cast Polypropylene) film. BOPP film is known for its excellent moisture barrier and clarity, making it ideal for packaging and labels. PET film offers high tensile strength and dimensional stability, suitable for food packaging and electronics. CPP film, characterized by its heat sealability and flexibility, is commonly used in food packaging and laminating applications. Each type serves distinct market needs while focusing on enhancing product durability and shelf appeal.</p></p>
