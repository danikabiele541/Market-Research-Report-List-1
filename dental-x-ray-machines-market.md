<p><p>The dental X-ray machines market comprises various types, primarily Ordinary X-rays and Panoramic X-rays. Ordinary X-rays, including bitewing and periapical radiographs, focus on specific teeth or areas, providing detailed images for diagnosing cavities, infections, and bone loss. Panoramic X-rays, on the other hand, capture a wide view of the entire mouth, including both dental arches and surrounding structures, aiding in comprehensive assessments and treatment planning. Each type serves distinct diagnostic purposes, enhancing dental care and patient outcomes.</p></p>
