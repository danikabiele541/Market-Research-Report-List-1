<p><p>Chromatography is a vital technique in biotechnology for separating and analyzing compounds. Gas Chromatography (GC) is ideal for volatile substances, while Thin Layer Chromatography (TLC) offers a simple, rapid method for qualitative analysis. Supercritical Fluid Chromatography (SFC) combines gas and liquid properties for efficient separation. Liquid Chromatography encompasses various methods, including High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (HPLC) and Ultra-High-Performance Liquid Chromatography (UHPLC), which provide high resolution and speed. Affinity Chromatography targets specific biomolecules, making it essential for purifying proteins and antibodies.</p></p>
