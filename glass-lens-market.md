<p><p>The glass lens market comprises several types, including progressive glass lenses, single vision lenses, and bifocal glass lenses. Progressive lenses offer a seamless transition between different focal points, catering to presbyopia without visible lines. Single vision lenses provide a uniform prescription for one distance, ideal for distance or reading tasks. Bifocal lenses feature two distinct vision zones, enabling users to switch between near and far vision. Each type serves specific visual needs, contributing to the diverse optical market landscape.</p></p>
