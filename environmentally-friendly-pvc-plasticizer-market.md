<p><p>The environmentally-friendly PVC plasticizer market comprises several key types: Adipates Esters, Trimellitates Esters, Epoxies Esters, and Benzoates. Adipates Esters are known for their low volatility and excellent low-temperature flexibility, making them suitable for applications like flexible packaging. Trimellitates Esters enhance heat stability and are ideal for high-performance products. Epoxies Esters provide superior durability and resistance to UV light, while Benzoates are often used for their low toxicity and good plasticizing properties, catering to a broader range of eco-conscious applications.</p></p>
