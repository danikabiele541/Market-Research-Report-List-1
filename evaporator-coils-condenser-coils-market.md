<p><p>Evaporator coils and condenser coils are essential components in HVAC systems. The market can be segmented into three main types. Finned or plated coils incorporate fins on tubes to enhance surface area, improving heat transfer rates. Combined coils merge both designs to optimize performance in space-constrained environments. Each type serves specific needs, influencing efficiency, cost, and installation in cooling and refrigeration systems.</p></p>
