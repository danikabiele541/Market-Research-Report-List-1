<p><strong>The Induction Holding Furnaces Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coreless Holding Furnaces</li><li>Channel Holding Furnaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Induction holding furnaces are essential for maintaining molten metal temperatures in foundries and manufacturing processes. Coreless holding furnaces utilize an induction coil to heat the metal directly, offering efficient energy use and precise temperature control. In contrast, channel holding furnaces have a dedicated channel that holds the liquid metal, benefiting large-scale operations by minimizing heat loss and enhancing melt consistency. Both types improve production efficiency and quality in metal casting and processing applications.</p></p>
