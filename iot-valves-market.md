<p><p>The IoT valves market encompasses various types designed for specific applications. Ball valves offer quick shut-off and precise flow control, making them ideal for on/off operations. Butterfly valves are lightweight and provide efficient flow regulation in large pipelines. Safety valves are crucial for pressure protection, automatically releasing excess pressure. Piston valves utilize linear motion for control and are suitable for high-pressure systems. Other variants may include solenoid and check valves, which cater to diverse fluid dynamics and automation needs in industrial settings.</p></p>
