<p><ul><li>Self-Propelled Belt Loader</li><li>Towed Belt Loader</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Belt Loader market consists of two primary types: Self-Propelled Belt Loaders and Towed Belt Loaders. Self-Propelled Belt Loaders are equipped with their own propulsion systems, allowing for greater maneuverability and efficiency during baggage handling at airports or freight terminals. In contrast, Towed Belt Loaders rely on another vehicle for movement, offering a cost-effective solution for operations with less frequent use or lower volume handling. Both types enhance logistical operations, ensuring smooth cargo transfer and optimizing turnaround times.</p></p>
