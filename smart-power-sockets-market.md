<p><p>Smart power sockets are categorized primarily into three types based on connectivity: Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and other technologies. Wi-Fi smart sockets allow remote control and monitoring via internet-enabled devices, enabling integration with smart home ecosystems. Bluetooth smart sockets offer local wireless control, typically within a limited range, ideal for smaller setups. Other market types may include Zigbee or Z-Wave sockets, which utilize mesh networks for reliable connectivity and lower energy consumption. Each type caters to varying user needs and preferences for automation and convenience.</p></p>
