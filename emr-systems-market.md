<p><p>EMR systems are categorized into cloud-based and on-premise markets. Cloud-based systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing for easier access, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, as users pay for what they need without heavy upfront investments. In contrast, on-premise systems are installed locally on a healthcare provider's infrastructure, offering greater control and customization but often requiring significant capital expenditure and ongoing maintenance. Each type presents distinct advantages and challenges, influencing healthcare providers' decision-making based on their specific needs and resources.</p></p>
