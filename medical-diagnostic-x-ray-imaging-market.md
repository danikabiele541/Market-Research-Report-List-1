<p><p>The medical diagnostic X-ray imaging market is divided into two primary types: stationary and portable X-rays. Stationary X-rays are fixed installations typically found in hospitals and clinics, offering high-quality imaging for comprehensive examinations. They are ideal for routine diagnostics and specialized imaging. In contrast, portable X-rays are compact units that can be easily transported, allowing for imaging in diverse settings, including emergency rooms and bedside applications. This flexibility enhances patient access to diagnostic services, especially in urgent or remote situations.</p></p>
