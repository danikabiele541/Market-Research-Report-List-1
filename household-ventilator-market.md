<p><p>The household ventilator market includes several types, primarily Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) devices, which provide constant airflow to keep airways open during sleep. Auto Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (Auto CPAP) adjusts pressure automatically based on the user's needs throughout the night. Other market types may include bilevel positive airway pressure (BiPAP) machines, which offer different pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation, as well as newer, innovative systems designed for specific respiratory conditions or patient preferences.</p></p>
