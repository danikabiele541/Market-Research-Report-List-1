<p><p>Traditional Zero Liquid Discharge (ZLD) Systems aim to eliminate liquid waste by recovering water and solid residues from industrial processes. The market is segmented into preprocessing, filtration, evaporation, and crystallization. Preprocessing involves initial treatment to prepare wastewater for ZLD. Filtration removes suspended solids and contaminants. Evaporation concentrates the liquid by transforming it into vapor, leaving behind residual salts. Finally, crystallization solidifies these salts for further disposal or reuse, ensuring minimal environmental impact and efficient resource recovery.</p></p>
