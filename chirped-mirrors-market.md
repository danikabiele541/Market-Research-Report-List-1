<p><p>Chirped mirrors are specialized optical components designed to manipulate and compress laser pulses. The market for these mirrors is segmented into rectangular and circular types. Rectangular mirrors are commonly used in applications requiring spatially uniform beam profiles, suitable for industrial and scientific lasers. In contrast, circular mirrors cater to applications needing rotational symmetry, often found in fiber laser systems and certain medical devices. Both types provide unique advantages in pulse shaping and dispersion management across various industries.</p></p>
