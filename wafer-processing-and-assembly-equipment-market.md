<p><p>The wafer processing and assembly equipment market encompasses various essential technologies for semiconductor manufacturing. Chemical Mechanical Polishing (CMP) equipment smooths surfaces to create uniform layers. Etching equipment selectively removes material to define patterns on wafers. Thin film deposition equipment applies layers of material, crucial for electronic device functionality. Photoresist processing equipment deposits light-sensitive materials to enable pattern transfer during lithography. Assembly equipment assembles and packages semiconductor components, ensuring proper functionality and protection for final products in the electronics industry.</p></p>
