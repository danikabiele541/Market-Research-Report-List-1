<p><p>Active Power Filters (APFs) are advanced electronic devices designed to enhance power quality in electrical systems by eliminating harmonic distortions and improving power factor. <p><p>Active Power Filters (APFs) are crucial for enhancing power quality by mitigating harmonics and improving overall system efficiency. The market is segmented into Shunt Active Power Filters, which inject compensating currents to neutralize disturbances, and Series Active Power Filters, which filter out harmonics by injecting voltage in series with the load. Hybrid Active Power Filters combine both shunt and series functionalities, offering a comprehensive solution. Each type addresses specific power quality issues, catering to diverse industrial and commercial applications.</p></p>
