Double screening employs two layers, enhancing sorting efficiency and allowing finer material separation. Triple screening, the most advanced, consists of three layers, optimizing the separation of materials into distinct sizes and types, making it suitable for complex sorting requirements. These types cater to varying industrial needs, enhancing productivity and operational effectiveness.</p></p>
