Convertible roofs transform a vehicle into an open-air experience, retracting completely to create a cabriolet effect. Large sunroofs, often spanning most of the roof area, enhance passenger comfort by offering expansive views and natural light. Each type caters to varying consumer preferences and enhances vehicle aesthetics and functionality.</p></p>
