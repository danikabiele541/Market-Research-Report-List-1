<p><ul><li>Digital Cable</li><li>Terrestrial Digital</li><li>Satellite Digital</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Smart Set-Top Box (STB) market encompasses three primary types: Digital Cable, Terrestrial Digital, and Satellite Digital. Digital Cable STBs provide access to cable television services via coaxial cables, offering a wide range of channels and on-demand content. Terrestrial Digital STBs receive signals over the air from broadcast towers, providing free-to-air television. Satellite Digital STBs access programming via satellite signals, enabling users to receive channels from various regions. Each type caters to different viewing preferences and infrastructure availability.</p></p>
