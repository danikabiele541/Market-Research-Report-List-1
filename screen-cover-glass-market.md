<p><p>The **Screen Cover Glass Market** includes **Synthetic Sapphire**, **Tempered Glass**, and **PMMA** as key materials. **Synthetic Sapphire** offers exceptional hardness and scratch resistance, ideal for premium devices but costly. **Tempered Glass** is widely used due to its balance of durability, affordability, and ease of production, making it suitable for most smartphones and tablets. **PMMA (Polymethyl Methacrylate)** is lightweight, cost-effective, and shatter-resistant, often used in budget-friendly devices but less scratch-resistant compared to sapphire or tempered glass. Each material caters to different market segments based on performance and cost.</p></p>
