<p><p>Roller screw actuators are found in two primary market types: standard and inverted. Standard roller screw actuators feature a conventional design where the screw moves in a linear path, offering high load capacities and efficiency for various applications. In contrast, inverted roller screw actuators have an upside-down configuration, allowing for greater compactness and lower profile designs, making them ideal for space-constrained environments. Both types provide enhanced performance and precision, catering to diverse industrial and technological needs.</p></p>
