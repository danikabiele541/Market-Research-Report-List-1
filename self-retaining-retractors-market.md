<p><ul><li>Single Use Type</li><li>Reusable Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The self-retaining retractors market is segmented into two primary types: single-use and reusable. Single-use retractors are designed for one-time use, promoting heightened hygiene and reducing the risk of cross-contamination, making them ideal for various surgical settings. In contrast, reusable retractors are built for longevity, allowing for multiple sterilization cycles, thereby offering cost savings over time. Both types cater to different surgical needs, with the choice dependent on factors like procedure type, cost considerations, and infection control protocols.</p></p>
