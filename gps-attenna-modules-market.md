<p><strong>The GPS Attenna Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active GPS Attenna Module</li><li>Passive GPS Attenna Module</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS antenna module market comprises two primary types: active and passive. Active GPS antenna modules integrate an amplifier, enhancing signal strength for improved reception, making them ideal for environments with weak signals. These modules require power to operate. In contrast, passive GPS antenna modules do not have built-in amplifiers and rely on the received signal's strength without requiring external power. These are typically used in applications where size and power consumption are critical, like compact devices.</p></p>
