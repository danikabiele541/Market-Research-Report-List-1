<p><ul><li>Cold Pressing</li><li>Hot Pressing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heavy bamboo flooring market involves two primary types of manufacturing processes: cold pressing and hot pressing. Cold pressing utilizes lower temperatures and pressure, preserving the bamboo's natural properties and resulting in a denser product with less environmental impact. In contrast, hot pressing involves higher temperatures and pressure, allowing for faster production and often yielding flooring with enhanced durability and a smoother finish. Both methods cater to different consumer preferences and applications, influencing the overall market dynamics.</p></p>
