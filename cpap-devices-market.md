<p><strong>The CPAP Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Pressure CPAP Device</li><li>Auto Adjusting CPAP Device</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CPAP devices market comprises two primary types: Fixed Pressure CPAP devices and Auto-Adjusting CPAP devices. Fixed Pressure CPAP devices deliver a constant air pressure set by a healthcare provider to keep the airway open during sleep. In contrast, Auto-Adjusting CPAP devices automatically adjust the air pressure throughout the night, responding to variations in airflow needs based on the user's breathing patterns. This flexibility can enhance comfort and effectiveness in treating sleep apnea, catering to individual patient requirements.</p></p>
