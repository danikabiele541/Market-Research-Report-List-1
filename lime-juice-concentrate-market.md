<p><p>The lime juice concentrate market is divided into two main types: frozen and preserved. Frozen lime juice concentrate is produced by freezing fresh lime juice, retaining its flavor and nutrients, making it ideal for long-term storage and convenient usage in various culinary applications. Preserved lime juice concentrate, on the other hand, undergoes processes such as pasteurization or addition of preservatives, allowing it to have a longer shelf life without refrigeration. Both types cater to different consumer preferences and usage scenarios.</p></p>
