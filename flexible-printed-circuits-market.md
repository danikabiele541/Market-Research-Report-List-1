<p><ul><li>Single Panel</li><li>Double Panel</li><li>Multilayer Panel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flexible printed circuits (FPC) market consists of three primary types: single panel, double panel, and multilayer panel. Single panels are the simplest, featuring a single layer of conductive pathways, ideal for basic applications. Double panels incorporate two layers, allowing for more complex circuitry and compact designs. Multilayer panels consist of multiple layers of interconnections, enabling high-density applications and improved functionality, often used in advanced electronics like smartphones and medical devices, providing greater versatility and efficiency.</p></p>
