<p><p>The TPU yarn market is segmented into one-component and two-component types. One-component TPU yarns are pre-reacted and require no additional materials for processing, making them convenient for various applications. In contrast, two-component TPU yarns consist of separate components that need to be mixed before use, offering flexibility in customization and enhanced properties. Both types cater to diverse industries, including textiles and automotive, where specific performance characteristics are essential for applications such as upholstery and footwear.</p></p>
