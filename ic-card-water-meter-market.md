<p><p>The IC Card Water Meter market is segmented into two primary types: hot water meters and cold water meters. Hot water meters are designed to measure water consumption at elevated temperatures, typically for heating systems and industries. In contrast, cold water meters are used for standard domestic and commercial water supply at lower temperatures. Both types utilize integrated circuit (IC) technology, allowing for efficient data management and billing through pre-paid or post-paid card systems, enhancing user convenience and accuracy.</p></p>
