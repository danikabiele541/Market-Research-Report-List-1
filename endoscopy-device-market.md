<p><p>The endoscopy device market is primarily categorized into flexible and rigid endoscopes. Flexible endoscopes are designed with a flexible tube, allowing them to navigate complex anatomical pathways, making them ideal for procedures in the gastrointestinal tract and respiratory system. Rigid endoscopes, on the other hand, have a straight structure and are commonly used for surgeries in areas like orthopedics and urology. Both types play crucial roles in diagnostic and therapeutic applications, enhancing visualization and precision during medical interventions.</p></p>
