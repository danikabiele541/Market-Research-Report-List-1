<p><ul><li>External Use</li><li>Internal Use</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The animal dewormer market is divided into two main types: external use and internal use. External use dewormers are applied topically to control parasites like fleas and ticks, primarily on pets and livestock. In contrast, internal use dewormers are administered orally or through injections to target internal parasites such as roundworms and tapeworms. Each segment caters to specific parasite control needs, influencing product formulations, administration methods, and target species within the veterinary market.</p></p>
