<p><p>The Semi-Compliant PTCA Balloon Dilatation Catheter market comprises two primary types: Conventional Balloons and High-Pressure Balloons. Conventional Balloons are designed to expand easily under low pressure, providing gentle dilation of narrowed arteries. In contrast, High-Pressure Balloons can withstand and operate at significantly elevated pressures, allowing for more aggressive treatment of stenosis and challenging lesions. Both types play crucial roles in percutaneous coronary intervention, enhancing patient outcomes by improving blood flow through obstructed vessels.</p></p>
