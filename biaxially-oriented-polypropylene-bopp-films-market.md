<p><p>Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene (BOPP) films are categorized based on thickness, influencing their applications in packaging and labeling. Films below 15 microns are lightweight and ideal for sachets and labels. The 15-30 micron range offers good strength and clarity, suitable for flexible packaging. Films between 30-45 microns provide enhanced durability for wraps and bags, while those above 45 microns are thicker, used in industrial applications and heavyweight packaging, offering superior mechanical properties and barrier functions.</p></p>
