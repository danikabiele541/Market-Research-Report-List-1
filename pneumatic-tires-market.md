<p><p>Pneumatic tires are categorized into Solid Pneumatics and Air Pneumatics. Solid Pneumatics are made of solid rubber, offering durability and resistance to punctures, ideal for heavy-duty applications like industrial vehicles. Air Pneumatics, filled with compressed air, provide better shock absorption, comfort, and traction, commonly used in automobiles and bicycles. While Solid Pneumatics excel in rugged environments, Air Pneumatics are preferred for smoother rides and fuel efficiency. Both types cater to diverse needs, balancing performance, durability, and application-specific requirements in the pneumatic tire market.</p></p>
