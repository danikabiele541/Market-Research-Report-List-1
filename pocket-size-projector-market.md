<p><strong>The Pocket-size Projector Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DLP</li><li>LCD</li><li>LCoS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pocket-size projector market features three main technologies: DLP, LCD, and LCoS. DLP (Digital Light Processing) uses micro mirrors for compact, vivid projection, ideal for portable devices. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) projects images using liquid crystals, offering consistent color and brightness but can be bulkier. LCoS (Liquid Crystal on Silicon) combines the benefits of both, providing high resolution and excellent color accuracy in a compact size, making it suitable for high-quality portable presentations and entertainment.</p></p>
