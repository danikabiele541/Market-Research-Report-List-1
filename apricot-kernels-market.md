<p><p>The apricot kernels market is primarily divided into two types: bitter and sweet. Bitter apricot kernels contain higher levels of amygdalin, which can produce toxins when metabolized, making them popular in alternative medicine and for producing extracts. In contrast, sweet apricot kernels have a lower amygdalin content and are often used in culinary applications, snacks, and health products. Both types cater to distinct consumer preferences and regulatory standards, influencing their market demand and pricing strategies.</p></p>
