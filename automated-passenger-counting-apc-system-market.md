<p><strong>The Automated Passenger Counting (APC) System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared Type</li><li>Time-of-Flight Type</li><li>Stereoscopic Vision Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automated Passenger Counting (APC) systems enhance transit efficiency by accurately measuring passenger numbers. Infrared type systems use infrared sensors to detect movements across a threshold, providing real-time counts. Time-of-Flight systems utilize laser technology to measure the distance to passengers, ensuring high precision in varied lighting conditions. Stereoscopic vision systems employ dual cameras to create depth perception, allowing for accurate counting even in crowded environments. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to diverse transit needs and enhancing operational planning.</p></p>
