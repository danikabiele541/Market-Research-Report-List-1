<p><p>Ultrasonic thickness gauges are classified into two main market types: AC and DC. AC gauges utilize alternating current to generate ultrasonic waves, allowing for precise measurements in various materials, especially in complex geometries. They excel in applications requiring rapid readings and surface preparation analysis. On the other hand, DC gauges operate with direct current, offering superior accuracy in thicker materials and enhanced performance in harsh environments. Both types serve critical roles in industries such as manufacturing, construction, and maintenance.</p></p>
