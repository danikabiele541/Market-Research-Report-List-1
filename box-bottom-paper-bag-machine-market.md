<p><p>The Box Bottom Paper Bag Machine market is primarily categorized into two types: Sheet-Fed and Roll-Fed paper bag machines. Sheet-Fed machines utilize pre-cut sheets of paper, allowing for precise control over bag dimensions and design, making them ideal for custom designs and smaller batches. In contrast, Roll-Fed machines work with continuous rolls of paper, enabling high-speed production and cost efficiency for larger volumes. Both types serve different market needs, balancing customization and production efficiency.</p></p>
