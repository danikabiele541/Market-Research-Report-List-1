<p><p>The Ceramic Fiber Blankets market encompasses two primary types: Ceramic Fiber Throw Blankets and Ceramic Fiber Spray Blankets. Ceramic Fiber Throw Blankets are lightweight, flexible insulation materials, often used in high-temperature applications such as furnaces and kilns, providing excellent thermal resistance. In contrast, Ceramic Fiber Spray Blankets are applied as a sprayable material, allowing for seamless insulation coverage on irregular surfaces, ideal for industrial settings. Both types are essential for energy efficiency and thermal management in various industries.</p></p>
