<p><p>Acrylonitrile Styrene Acrylate (ASA) copolymers are categorized primarily into three market types: Extrusion Grade, Injection Grade, and Others. Extrusion Grade ASA is designed for processes such as film and sheet extrusion, offering enhanced durability and weather resistance. Injection Grade ASA, on the other hand, is tailored for injection molding applications, providing excellent flow characteristics and mechanical strength. The "Others" category includes specialized grades for niche applications, highlighting the versatility of ASA in various manufacturing processes and industries.</p></p>
