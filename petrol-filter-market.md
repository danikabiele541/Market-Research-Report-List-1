<p><strong>The Petrol Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Carburetor</li><li>EFI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The petrol filter market is primarily categorized into two types: Carburetor and Electronic Fuel Injection (EFI) systems. Carburetor filters are used in older vehicles with carbureted engines, where fuel is mixed with air before entering the engine. In contrast, EFI systems utilize sophisticated technology to inject fuel directly into the combustion chamber, requiring high-efficiency filters to maintain optimal engine performance. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring clean fuel delivery, thereby enhancing engine efficiency and longevity.</p></p>
