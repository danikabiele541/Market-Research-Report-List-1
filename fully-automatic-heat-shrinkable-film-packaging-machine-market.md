<p><p>Fully automatic heat shrinkable film packaging machines are essential in the packaging industry, categorized primarily into two types: Vertical Form Fill Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form Fill Seal (H-FFS). V-FFS machines produce bags from a roll of film, filling and sealing them vertically, making them efficient for lightweight products. In contrast, H-FFS machines utilize a horizontal format, ideal for larger or heavier items, enabling simultaneous filling and sealing for effective packaging solutions across diverse industries.</p></p>
