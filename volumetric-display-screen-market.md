<p><strong>The Volumetric Display Screen Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Static Volume</li><li>Swept Volume</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The volumetric display screen market comprises two main types: static volume and swept volume displays. Static volume displays present three-dimensional visuals that remain fixed in space, allowing viewers to observe images from various angles without motion. In contrast, swept volume displays generate dynamic 3D visuals by moving images through a designated space, creating the illusion of depth and interaction. Together, these technologies cater to diverse applications in fields like medicine, entertainment, and simulation, enhancing user experience through immersive visualizations.</p></p>
