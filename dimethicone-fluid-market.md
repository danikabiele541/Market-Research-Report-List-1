<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity Dimethicone</li><li>Medium Viscosity Dimethicone</li><li>High Viscosity Dimethicone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dimethicone fluid market is categorized into three types based on viscosity: low, medium, and high. Low viscosity dimethicone is lightweight and easily spreadable, making it suitable for formulations like lotions and serums. Medium viscosity dimethicone offers a balance between spreadability and thickness, commonly used in creams and conditioners. High viscosity dimethicone provides a thicker texture, often utilized in products requiring added stability and enhanced barrier properties, such as hair styling gels and intensive moisturizers. Each type serves specific cosmetic and personal care applications.</p></p>
