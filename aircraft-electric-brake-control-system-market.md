<p><p>The Aircraft Electric Brake Control System market is categorized into single-disc, dual-disc, and multiple-disc brake systems. Single-disc brakes utilize one disc per wheel, offering simplicity and weight advantages, ideal for smaller aircraft. Dual-disc brakes feature two discs per wheel, enhancing braking performance and heat dissipation, suitable for medium aircraft. Multiple-disc brakes employ several discs, providing superior braking force and are commonly used in large, high-performance aircraft, where efficiency and safety are paramount during landing and takeoff operations.</p></p>
