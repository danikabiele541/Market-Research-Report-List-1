<p><p>Pusher kilns are used for the continuous firing of ceramic ware and come in two main market types: manual and automatic. Manual pusher kilns require operators to control the firing process, including loading and unloading materials, which allows for greater hands-on adjustment but may lead to variability in quality. In contrast, automatic pusher kilns utilize advanced technology and automation, offering consistent firing conditions and improved efficiency, thus enabling higher production rates with less labor intervention. Both types cater to different production needs and operational capabilities.</p></p>
